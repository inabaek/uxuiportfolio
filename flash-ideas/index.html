"Food Tracer"

I came up with this idea concerning food waste issue

*This video contains background music

My back-story

I had a chance to watch a documentary related to the environmental issue. Then I learned one of the huge problems is the food waste we make.
This leads me to think about how much food waste I make in a day. which I wouldn't have been concerned about before. I was surprised at how much food waste I make through a day, week, and month! It was much more than I thought.
Then I started to think what if there is an app that can help us reduce food waste.

Target users

Grocery customers

Reserch data

(Data from WRAP)

- UK households (2018) throw away 6.6 million tonnes of household food waste a year in the UK, compared to 8.1 million tonnes in 2007.
- Of the 6.6 million tonnes we throw away, almost three quarters (70% of the total) is food we could have eaten (4.5 million tonnes)
- Estimated annual food waste arisings within UK households, hospitality & food service (HaFS), food manufacture, retail and wholesale sectors in 2018 at around 9.5 million tonnes, 70% of which was intended to be consumed by people (30% being the ‘inedible parts’).
[fun facts – based on 2018 data]
The food and drink we waste, that could have been eaten (the 4.5 million tonnes) would fill:
  • 8 Wembley Stadiums (London, UK)
  • 90 Royal Albert Halls
  • 38 million wheelie bins (based on a standard 240l)
  • 3,600 Olympic sized swimming pools
  • 490,000 bin lorries/dustcarts

The idea

Too much food waste...  What should we do with this problem?
I came up with the idea of tracing our food we buy to reduce food waste.
The idea is like this :
  Step 1. Buy food items in a store.
  Step 2. Scan the Items.
  Step 3. The scanner will send you a list of the products with every detail such as used by date, ingredients, etc.
  Step 4. You can check what you have in your kitchen at a glance on your phone.
  Step 5. The app will send you a notification when the food item is due to expired.
  Step 6. The app will recommend food recipes with what you have in stock.
  Step 7. The app will show you a graph of how much you have saved or waste food this day/week/month.
  Step 8. Feel proud of yourself!

The benefit

Using this app gives valuable benefits to you and the environment!
  ✔ No printed receipt required (save energy + time + environment)
  ✔ Prevent producing food waste (save energy + money + environment)
  ✔ Feel more confident about meal plan (save time + food +environment)
  ✔Contribute to saving the earth!
