Overview

Option 1

[Spending time on social media]

Option 2

[Spending time with your loved one]

Imagine you and your loved one are sitting on a sofa at home. Now, you have two options to spend time.
One, spending time on social media such as Instagram, YouTube and Tic-Tok and etc. Two, having a meaningful conversation and make special memory between you and your husband. What would you choose to do?
Let's say you decided to make a conversation with your loved one. But the thing is you don't know where to start.
Therefore, we decided to make a 'Get To Know You' question cards game app. So that you can make a meaningful and special memory with your loved ones.

Problem Statement

The smartphone is providing endless useful products to humans than ever these days. The smartphone even connects people through social media, thanks to smartphones. At the same time, human loneliness is a huge growing social problem because of smartphones but we still use them.
My solution should provide a better and meaningful product to people so that it alleviates loneliness.

Define

Qualitative data

According to Socialsci, communication with others keeps us healthier. It affects people's mental and physical health directly. For example, it has been shown that people who have a disease such as cancer, depression, and even the common cold, can alleviate their symptoms simply by communicating with others.

Discover

Competitor A

A virtual expansion pack for fill-in-the-blank style card games.

Pros

Contents
- Humerus questions /great game to play with friends.
Function
- A shuffle cards function.
- Create customized questions.
- Progress bar to show how many cards are left

Cons

Description
- A lack of user guide and information about the application.
Contents
- Very limited free cards available.
Ad
- Too many adds pop up.

Unique Point

Creat own question cards.

Competitor B

A question game where you say "Yes" or "No" to casual, personal, challenging and crazy questions and immediately see how other players answered. Perfect for parties as well as for just passing time.

Pros

Great feedback
- User can see the other players' answers by percentage
- Nice feedback on clicking events

Function

- Progress bar to show how many cards are left
Action
- Share the question on social media which the user liked.

Cons

Lack of description
- A lack of user guide and information about the application.
Poor UI feature on some parts
- Even though the app has many great gaming elements, 'Comments' part seems it needs final touch.

Unique Point

- Interesting gaming elements such as earn coins by doing some tasks, buy coins and etc.
- See other players' answers

Competitor C

It's icebreaker game for almost any occasion. Whether on a first date or a nice evening with friends.

Pros

Clear design
- Easy to understand how the app runs at a glance
Language choices
- Variety Language choices
More game choices by the same game developers
- There are 5 other different games available

Cons

Lack of feedback
- Not showing how many cards are left to play
Too much ads
- Every few cards pop-up ads appears and disturbs the game

Unique Point

More choices of party games
