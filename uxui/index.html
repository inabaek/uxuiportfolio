Conversation card game app

February/2021 - Ongoing | Personal project

"Good conversation makes the moment special"

My friend and I went on a trip. I brought a conversation card game called Table-tok. There are 140 question cards in a box. The questions made us talk about a wide variety of things and I got to know my friend better through the conversations. After playing the game my friend and I thought what if we make the conversation card game as a digital product? So that we do not have to carry the cards around and play the game wherever we go! After the trip, I researched conversation games in general, and the research led me to think about how we might make the game more engaging and attractive? What makes an enjoyable user experience?

Overview

Option 1

[Spending time on social media]

Option 2

[Spending time with your loved one]

Imagine you and your loved one are sitting on a sofa at home. Now, you have two options to spend time. One, spending time on social media such as Instagram, YouTube and Tic-Tok and etc. Two, having a meaningful conversation and make special memory between you and your loved ones. What would you choose to do? Let's say you decided to make a conversation with your loved one. But the thing is you don't know where to start. Therefore, we decided to make a 'Get To Know You' question card game app. So that you can make a meaningful and special memory with your loved ones.

User Research

Findings from user research

• Conversation with others keeps us healthier.
• It affects people's mental and physical health directly.
• People tend to socialise and enjoy deep conversation with others.
• Many people feel connected if they have a deep conversation.
• More then half of respondents responded they would try a conversation game app.
• Many of them like to play with in couple or friend/friends.

Target Users

• Ages: 18-39
• Who often plays board/mobile game
• Who enjoys having a conversation

User needs

• Entertainment
Users are looking for a enjoyable game occasionally
• Conversation guide
Sometimes users find it difficult to get into a good conversation
• Understanding one's mind
Users need to understand his/her friend, partner, family, etc. by having a conversation

Hypotheses

• If we provide a variety of topics and its levels user can choose from, user can play different cards for next play.

• Providing the best-fit questions to user's situation based on qualitative data.

Competitor Research

Findings from competitor research

• Most of digital card games are about fun quiz and humerus questions for parties
• Simple design and easy to use
• Too many pop-up ads
• Gaming elements such as buying coins
• Very limited cards to play
