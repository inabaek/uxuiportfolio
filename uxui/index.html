Project summery

Because of the Covid-19, people spend most of their time at home. Whether you are living with family or friends, it is a perfect time to have a deep and meaningful conversation and feel connected to each other. But the thing is you don't know where to start. Therefore, we decided to make a 'Get To Know You' question cards game app.
The topics cover :
- Life
- Relationship
- Careers
- Hobby
- Childhood
This card game app also can be used for the icebreaking conversation when you first meet people to get to know each other. For example, in the education sector such as schools, collages, etc.
