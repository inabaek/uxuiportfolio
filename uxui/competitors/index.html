Competitor research

3 different competitors

Findings from competitor research

• Most of digital card games are about fun quiz and humerus questions for parties
• Simple design and easy to use
• Too many pop-up ads
• Gaming elements such as buying coins
• Very limited cards to play

Competitor A

A virtual expansion pack for fill-in-the-blank style card games.

Pros

Contents
- Humerus questions /great game to play with friends.
Function
- A shuffle cards function.
- Create customized questions.
- Progress bar to show how many cards are left

Cons

Description
- A lack of user guide and information about the application.
Contents
- Very limited free cards available.
Ad
- Too many ads pop up.

Unique Point

Creat own question cards.

Competitor B

A question game where you say "Yes" or "No" to casual, personal, challenging and crazy questions and immediately see how other players answered. Perfect for parties as well as for just passing time.

Pros

Great feedback
- User can see the other players' answers by percentage
- Nice feedback on clicking events
Function
- Progress bar to show how many cards are left
Action
- Share the question on social media which the user liked.

Cons

Lack of description
- A lack of user guide and information about the application.
Poor UI feature on some parts
- Even though the app has many great gaming elements, 'Comments' part seems it needs final touch.

Unique Point

- Interesting gaming elements such as earn coins by doing some tasks, buy coins and etc.
- See other players' answers

Competitor C

It's icebreaker game for almost any occasion. Whether on a first date or a nice evening with friends.

Pros

Clear design
- Easy to understand how the app runs at a glance
Language choices
- Variety Language choices
More game choices by the same game developers
- There are 5 other different games available

Cons

Lack of feedback
- Not showing how many cards are left to play
Too much ads
- Every few cards pop-up ads appears and disturbs the game

Unique Point

More choices of other games (Their other game apps)
