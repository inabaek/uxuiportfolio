Papii Website Project / Brunch cafe in Newtown, Edinburgh

Papii is one of the most popular brunch cafes in Edinburgh. I reached out to Papii to see if they need a website for their business. Luckily, my client wanted to make a website for his business but did not know where to start. So I offered to make one for him. It is an ongoing project. I hope this website project goes well so that I can reach my clients' expectations as well as providing a better service to potential users!

User Survey

Findings from user survey

• User's goals to visit the cafe website are to get some information about opening hours, menu, location, and some pictures of a cafe.
• Users find it helpful if a cafe has its website.
• Most of the users use a phone for checking the website.
• Some users checking the website if there is any discount or special offer.

Findings from Personas

The main targets for Papii are office people, tourists, and students. I made each persona of Papii's main target so that I can understand them better.

• Many young professionals are working around the cafe. They want some food to take away for their lunchtime.
• Many tourists visit Papii. Tourists want to find out the information about Papii so they can decide if they want to visit Papii.
• University students want to hang out in town. They want to explore places they can go and hang out with friends but can't decide where to go.

Creating Papii's new logo and menu

The client asked me if I could create a new logo and menu for their business while I was making their website. I have experience making some logos and menu boards in the past so I accepted the offer.
The client wanted the design to be related to the Greece theme since 'Papii' is the Greek word meaning a duck. I researched some fresh and beachy images of Greece and came up with a few that the client might find interesting for reference.
Once we agreed on this theme, I created the logo and the menu.
To make a menu board, we needed a photographer to take some pictures of food.
He did a great job.
