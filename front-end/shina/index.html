Clarinetist Shina's Website Project

Shina is a clarinetist who began playing the clarinet at the age of 13.
She used to actively playing clarinet with other musicians. However, because of the Covid-19 restriction, it was hard to meet in person. This made her a very limited community in her field.
I thought it would be a good idea to make her a website so that she can reach out to other musicians in a well-presented way. Or it would be helpful when other musicians want to reach out to Shina.
Also, it can be used as an advertising tool for her private online music lessons.
This project is ongoing and I am very excited to see this website is being used by Shina soon!
